From Chicago grew a business professional who revels in potential, a love for the pursuit of excellence and an inspiration towards art and creativity. I am Demetrius Williamson - marketing professional, writer, avid traveler & progressive thinker. One-time professional athlete, now a diverse business visionary. A creative with history in executing outstanding concepts and ideas of all shapes and sizes within brand marketing, business development & project management.

You will find at glance my sophistication, diligence and personality to be one favorable for a culturally driven environment. Excellence began with an athletic foundation. Native of Chicago, I am the grandson of Albert Williamson, a professional Negro League Baseball player. I traveled the world young playing organized basketball from the Netherlands to Australia. Playing out my college career at NCAA Division I institutions, University of Missouri and Niagara University. Finishing a professional stint in Tianjin, China.

Beyond the basketball court, my background is marketing driven, emphasizing strategy, branding and storytelling. Pushing the culture and telling the most inspiring stories. I worked for nearly a decade at Union Pacific, a Fortune 500 company. Spearheading an environment away from tradition into cultural relevance. The most recent title I held there was Senior Network Marketing Manager. I’ve also collaborated for close to 5 years pro bono with Jordan Brand on marketing strategies and treatments.

A brief background of previous positions held: Sr. Network Marketing Manager Sr. Premium Accounts Manager Manager of Territory Operation

My native understanding of business is truly from obsessing the work, researching and crafting what pushes the needle and explode boundaries. My prowess has a range from professional athlete to exceptional performance in business development, relationship building & brand marketing.

Sports and athletics remain a cornerstone for who I am. My passion expands within branding, storytelling, fashion, photography, music and art. I’m looking to pursue culturally driven projects that merge my talents and create the best work of my life! Let’s collaborate!
